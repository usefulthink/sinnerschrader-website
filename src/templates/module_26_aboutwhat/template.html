WE HAVE 7 SACRED RULES
TO BUILDING A GREAT
TRANSFORMATIONAL PRODUCT:
1

Be Human

User. First. Period.

We put the human being first and centre. What we design solves real problems for real people.
This razor-sharp focus is absolutely essential. Without it, you get inevitably lost in lots and lots of possibilities.

2

Be Ambitious

Add 10x more value than now.

Don’t increment. Jump to the next level. Transformational products change the life of your customers for the better. By adding a lot more value, compared to what they currently use. Aim for a tenfold increase. At least.

3

Be Simple

Inspire with user experiences.

What the user experiences IS your product. Nothing else. Start with the customer experience and work backwards to the technology, as Steve Jobs famously put it. Be inspiring. Be delightful. Be simple.

4

Be Mass

Scale to all users and markets.

It’s perfectly fine to start in a niche. But then you have to grow fast and scale your product beyond all borders, be it consumer segments or regional markets. All people on planet Earth are your potential users.

5

Be Possible

Grow into a platform ecosystem.

Think in platforms and ecosystems right from the beginning. The first iteration of your product might not be a platform. But at least it should have the built-in potential to grow into a platform ecosystem.

6

Be Open

Connect APIs and business opportunities.

Deliver value through all possible channels. Make your platform available to third-party developers. Find business models that allow growth through creative partnerships. Sell whatever you have developed, including access to your platform.

7

Be Influential

Be the marketing hub.

You need to own your market. And your marketing. Close the marketing loop. Be your own marketing machine and design marketing right into your product. Make your customers your best ambassadors.
